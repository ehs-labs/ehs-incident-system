# Worker guide

## What you can do

- **Submit incidents** from your assigned site(s)
- **View your own incidents** and their progress
- **Complete corrective actions** assigned to you

## Submitting an incident

1. Click **+ New Incident** from the dashboard or `/incidents/new`
2. Fill in:
   - Site (defaults to your primary site if you have one)
   - Type — pick the closest match
   - When it happened — `occurred_at` defaults to now
   - Where — a free-text location within the site
   - Summary — one sentence
   - Description — what happened, in your own words
   - Severity hint — your sense of how serious it is (the investigator may adjust)
3. Add witnesses (name + statement)
4. Attach photos — drag-and-drop, up to 25 MB each
5. Click **Submit**

You'll receive an email and an in-app notification when the investigator triages it.

## Completing an assigned action

1. Go to **Actions** (sidebar) — your open actions are highlighted
2. Click an action to open the detail view
3. Click **Start** to move it to "In progress"
4. Do the work in the real world
5. Return to the app; add a short note about what you did; attach evidence if applicable
6. Click **Mark Done**

The investigator will verify and close it.

## Profile

- **Email** — change requires re-confirmation
- **Password** — change requires current password
- **Telegram** — link your chat to receive notifications via Telegram bot
